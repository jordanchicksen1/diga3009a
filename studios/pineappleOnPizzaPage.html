An acquired taste.

This studio focuses on creating endlessly replyable, arcade-style mobile games that are immediately intuitive. However, much like other South African developers, they like to encorporate South African themes and aesthetics into their games. In a sense, they digitize South African culture.

Location: Braamfontein, Johannesburg

Founded: 2022

Speciality: 3D Mobile games

Notable works: AmaTaxi, Braai Guy Origins, POP Invaders
