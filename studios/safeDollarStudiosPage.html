Humble beginnings.

After the Campus Game Jam 2024, a small group of Wits students decided to form their own little game studio. Whilst they are still figuring themselves out and finding out what is their lane, SDS aims to provide engaging, understandable to all and (most importantly) fun gameplay experience to people of all ages and walks of life. At the moment, they are in a testing phase, where they are trying to push out as many prototypes as possible to see what resonates with their supporters. They focus on smaller ideas or experiences and navigate how those things can fit within a larger picture.

Location: Braamfontein, Johannesburg

Founded: 2024

Speciality: Collections of smaller ideas

Notable works: Colour Chase
