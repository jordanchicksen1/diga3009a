Connection through the digital realm.

This studio makes visually striking games with "chunky" design. Started by a couple of friends in Stellenbosch, this studio is fully online and is quite relaxed with how their work is structured. This allows their team to create intricately designed experiences in their own time, giving each project as much time and attention as it needs.

Location: Stellenbosch, Cape Town

Founded: 2014

Speciality: 2D games

Notable works: Upupa Bird Ninja, Jetstream, Terra Nil
