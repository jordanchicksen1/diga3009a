Chaotic harmony!

Acquired by Reforged Studios in 2024, this studio has produced an interesting take on the idea of a "music game", in the sense thattheir game has a keen focus on music and encorporates music into the game mechanics, but it is not a rhythym game. This displays the studio's keen ability to subvert expectations whilst still delivering an impactful experience.

Location: Cape Town, South Africa
