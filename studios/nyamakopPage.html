A force for good.

Based in Rosebank, Johannesburg. Comprised of South Africans from across the country, with many coming from University of the Witwatersrand. Nyamakop also supports the independent game development scene in Johannesburg, as they help to fund many game jams, participate in the monthly Joburg Game Dev Meetups and other South African game-related events.
