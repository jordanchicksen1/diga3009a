For the culture!

This studio values African culture and mythology, and embeds that games that are influenced by tried and true game archetypes and styles (like Gradius and Hades). Their blend of new and old ideas recontextualizes what their audience has known before, to create something that feels new yet familiar.

Location: Randburg, Johannesburg

Founded: 2023

Speciality: Gaming nostalgia with an African twist

Notable works: Super Diski FC, Reclaim, Khamani and The Arena of The Gods

Website: Disputed People Games
