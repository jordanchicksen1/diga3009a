Plant. Climb. Upgrade. Repeat.

Reclaim (2025) is an expressive platforming roguelike where players must bring life back to a barren world. By platforming in the space, players will plant seeds and grow greenery. After each run, players can upgrade their character's stats to make each run more effective than the last.
