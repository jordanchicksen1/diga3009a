Defeat the demons as fast as you can!

Neon White (2022) is a fastpaced first-person shooter, with a focus on platforming and speedrunning. Fire your weapons until they are depleted, our discard them to activate a special ability. Get through the levels as fast as possible to earn high ranks. If you're fast enough, your times will be placed on the leaderboard.
