Death is not the end.

Pecking Order (???) is an upcoming multiplayer about a war between birds and worms. Collect resources and try to survive as worms, and try to eat as many worms as possible as birds. However, dying doesn't mean game over in this game, it means switching sides. Dying as worm turns you into a bird, and vice versa. Experience the food chain and circle of life in real time!
