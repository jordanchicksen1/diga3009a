Braai Guy Origins (2023) is an arcade roguelite shooter where players must fend off evil braais by shooting steaks at them. Survive as long as possible and complete levels to gain upgrades, such as extra damage, increased damage speed, extra hit points and more! Don't stop until every braai had been braai'd!
