Become the greatest taxi driver!

Taxi Ranked (2023) is an arcade-style driving game where players must pick up and drop off passengers of a taxi. However, its not that simple, as it is based off of South African roads. This means that players must avoid potholes and other roadworks, and evade police who are looking for bribes. Use money gained from passengers to customize your ride and perform well in each level to rank up on the leaderboard!
