About Us

South African Games Legacy is a site focused on celebrating and showcasing the creativity, effort, and talent within South Africa’s video game industry. From new indie studios to globally recognized developers, this site's goal is to highlight the teams and stories behind the country’s growing game development industry. With this goal, we aim to show users what they could aspire to create, or display what our country is capable of.

This site is built by passionate local game developers and enthusiasts who believe that South Africa deserves a spotlight in the global gaming landscape. We aim to connect players, developers, and supporters to strengthen our local gaming community. We would appreciate any feedback, whether you would like to suggest a game to be highlighted, or a change in the structure of the site.
